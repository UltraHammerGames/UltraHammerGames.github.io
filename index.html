WHAT IS ULTRA HAMMER GAMES?

Founded in Ontario, Canada, we are an independent studio that specializes in small-scale, replayable PC games. With an emphasis on passion and creativity, we aim to shine a long overdue spotlight on talented independent Canadian developers. Along with our projects, we strive to foster an inclusive community of equally expressive and vibrant members!

Interested in getting more familiar with our community? Be sure to follow our various social media platforms for news and content, and join our Discord server to interact with fellow members!

We hope you enjoy what you play!
